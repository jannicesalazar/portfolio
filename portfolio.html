About Me

Hello, my name is Jannice.

I am the daughter of an immigrant, proudly born and raised in the United States, and I spend much of my time in my beautiful home country of Costa Rica.

I have always loved people, and that love is at the heart of why I’m drawn to UI/UX design. My life has been shaped by community, empathy, and kindness. In Costa Rica, people center their lives around making others feel welcome and happy — and that philosophy has deeply influenced the way I think about design.

I believe great UI/UX is rooted in understanding people. I approach my work by prioritizing what is best for users and clients alike, aiming to create experiences that feel intuitive, accessible, and effortless. My goal is to reduce friction, create clarity, and design solutions that genuinely make people’s lives easier.
